摘要
二十世紀前葉，印順導師（1906-2005）所提出的「人間佛教」思想，承接太虛大師（1890-1947）「人生佛教」的佛教改革理念，強調佛教的「此時、此地、此人」等人間性特徵，戰後印順導師來到台灣，成立佛學院與寫作《妙雲集》、《華雨集》等著作，對戰後台灣佛教界的思想與文化影響甚鉅。解嚴以後，印順導師的學生各自開枝散葉，逐漸形成「印順學派」，他們對印順導師人間佛教思想的傳承與實踐，往往成為教界與社會的關注焦點。
在印順學派中，昭慧法師（1957-）與性廣法師（1962-）因其在宗教界、學術界、社會界都有十分重要的影響力，而成為印順導師「人間佛教思想」極為重要的闡發者與實踐者。在宗教界，她們因為創建佛教弘誓學院、提倡比丘尼的平等地位、重新闡釋戒律、與發展人間佛教禪法而昭著於世；在學術界，她們因著舉辦印順思想研討會、接任玄奘大學的重要職務、發表多部學術著作而對於「印順學」的建立與發展功不可沒，也對印順導師的「人間佛教思想」持續在學界發揮影響力產生了關鍵性的作用；在社會界，解嚴迄今，她們因著參與護教運動、動物保育、人權運動、反賭博、反核運動、同性婚姻等議題，不僅在政治、社運、媒體圈都結下許多善緣，也親身示範了人間佛教思想能為佛教界帶來的社會影響力，以及對社會的批判與反思。
故本研究以昭慧法師與性廣法師的思想與行動為核心，將兩位法師作為「行動思想家」的典範，探討兩位法師如何透過傳承印順導師的「人間佛教思想」，如何重塑尼師在當代台灣佛教的中的角色定位；並透過實踐「人間佛教思想」，如何為台灣佛教的現代化走出一條新的路徑。
關鍵字：人間佛教、太虛大師、印順導師、昭慧法師、性廣法師
Abstract
In the early twentieth century, Master Yin-shun (1906-2005) advanced the concept of “Buddhism for Human Realm,” building upon Master Tai-xu’s (1890-1947) reformist vision of “Buddhism for Human Life.” Emphasizing the human-centered characteristics of “this time, this place, and these people,” Yin-shun’s thought profoundly influenced the intellectual and cultural development of postwar Taiwanese Buddhism through his establishment of Buddhist colleges and the publication of key works such as The Collection of Miao-yun and The Collection of Hua-yu. Following Taiwan’s political liberalization in the late 1980s, Yin-shun’s students began to branch out independently, gradually forming the so-called “Yin Shun School,” whose efforts to inherit and implement Buddhism for Human Realm have become focal points in both religious and public spheres.
Among these disciples, Venerable Chao-hwei (b. 1957) and Venerable Shing-kuang (b. 1962) have emerged as two of the most prominent interpreters and practitioners of Yin-shun’s Buddhism for Human Realm, wielding significant influence in religious, academic, and social contexts. In the religious sphere, they are renowned for co-founding the Buddhist Hongshi Institute, promoting the equal status of bhikkhunis, reinterpreting the Vinaya, and developing Buddhism for Human Realm meditation systems. Academically, they have played a crucial role in the institutionalization of “Yin Shun Studies” by organizing conferences, holding key administrative positions at Hsuan Chuang University, and publishing a wide range of scholarly works. Socially, since the lifting of martial law, their active participation in campaigns for religious freedom, animal protection, human rights, anti-gambling and anti-nuclear movements, and same-sex marriage rights has not only built extensive alliances in political, social, and media circles, but also powerfully demonstrated the social impact and critical potential of Buddhism for Human Realm.
This thesis takes Venerables Chao-hwei and Shing-kuang as exemplary “engaged thinkers” and investigates how their interpretation and transmission of Master Yin-shun’s Buddhism for Human Realm have reshaped the role of bhikkhunis in contemporary Taiwanese Buddhism. Furthermore, it explores how their religious practice and social engagement have contributed to opening a new path for the modernization of Taiwanese Buddhism.
Keywords: Buddhism for Human Realm, Master Tai-xu , Master Yin-shun, Venerable Chao-hwei, Venerable Shing-kuang
